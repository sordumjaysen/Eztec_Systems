1. Introduction

EZTEC SYSTEMS ("we", "us", or "our") operates the TraderBook application — a business management platform for micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs) across Africa. TraderBook is available on Android, iOS, and as a web application, accessible via a single unified account.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use and protect it, and what rights you have as a user. By registering for or using TraderBook, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

We are committed to complying with applicable data protection laws, including the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA), the Benin Republic Digital Code (Law No. 2017-20, as amended by Law No. 2020-35) and Law No. 2009-09 on Protection of Personally Identifiable Information, and the Ghana Data Protection Act 2012 (Act 843).

EZTEC SYSTEMS, registered in Nigeria under the Companies and Allied Matters Act with registration number RC - 9533745, is the data controller responsible for your personal data processed through TraderBook.

6. How We Store and Protect Your Data

6.1 Storage Architecture

TraderBook uses a hybrid storage model:

  • Cloud (Supabase): Data is synced to Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure to enable seamless access across Android, iOS, and the web application.

6.2 Security Measures

We implement the following technical and organisational security measures:

  • PIN-based access control for local data
  • SHA-256 hashing for PIN storage
  • Encrypted data transmission over HTTPS/TLS
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies on Supabase
  • Token-based authentication via Supabase Auth
  • Restricted staff access based on assigned roles
