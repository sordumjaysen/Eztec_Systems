Why Join Us

This isn't
a job. It's a
founding moment.

West Africa is at an inflection point. The businesses, the talent, the capital — it's all here. What's been missing is world-class digital infrastructure built by people who understand the terrain. That's what we're building.

01

You'll own real outcomes

We don't hire people to fill boxes. Every person on the team owns a surface — from code to go-to-market to partnerships. Your decisions have visible consequences, fast.

02

You'll work across four verticals

TraderBook. ALORA. TALVÉRA. AHIMÍN. You'll gain exposure to product, fintech, real estate, and talent markets simultaneously — breadth most careers don't offer in a decade.

03

The mission is real

There are ~44 million SMEs in Nigeria and Benin who lack access to reliable business tools, verified talent, and trusted property markets. We're fixing that. The work matters.

04

You'll grow with the ecosystem

As the platforms scale, so do the roles. The people who join now are the ones who'll be leading as Eztec expands into Côte d'Ivoire, Ghana, and beyond.

Our Values

How we work.

Culture isn't what you put on a wall. It's the decisions you make when no one's watching. These are ours.

01

Context first, tools second

We study how people actually live, trade, and transact in Lagos and Cotonou before we write a single line of code. Software that ignores local context is just noise dressed as progress.

02

Ship, learn, iterate

We move with urgency. A version in users' hands teaches us more than a perfect spec in a meeting. We build fast, break things carefully, and improve relentlessly.

03

Design is not optional

We believe West African users deserve world-class product design. Not as a luxury. As a baseline. Every interface we ship goes through the same rigour you'd expect from Lagos, Tokyo, or London.

04

Small team, full trust

We don't micromanage. We hire people we trust and then we trust them. If you need to be watched to do good work, we're probably not the right fit — and we're happy to say so.

05

Speak plainly, disagree openly

The best idea wins, regardless of who brought it. We don't do politics. We do directness, good faith, and the willingness to change our minds when the evidence says to.

06

Build for the long game

We're not chasing a quick exit. We're building infrastructure for a generation of West African entrepreneurs. That means decisions made for the next decade, not the next funding round.

Hiring Process

Fast, honest, no games.

We respect your time. Our process is designed to move quickly and give you a clear signal at every stage.

Step 01
01

Send your email

No cover letter templates. Write us two paragraphs: what you've built and why Eztec. Include any links that matter — GitHub, portfolio, Figma files.

Step 02
02

Founder call

A direct 30-minute conversation with the Eztec founder. Not HR. Not a recruiter. We move fast — this usually happens within 3–5 business days.

Step 03
03

A real task

A short paid task or portfolio review relevant to the role. Nothing that takes more than a few hours. We pay for your time because we respect it.

Step 04
04

Offer

Clear terms. Honest compensation. No lowball-and-negotiate. We make an offer we'd be proud to share publicly and you decide.
