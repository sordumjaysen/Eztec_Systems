Who We Are

A Connected
Ecosystem.

Our model is simple — every product solves a distinct problem, and every product makes the others more powerful. A trader on TraderBook is one tap away from selling on Ahimín. A business profile built on one platform carries trust across the entire ecosystem.

Technology

Infrastructure Built
to Scale.

A production-grade stack chosen for reliability, offline resilience, and regional performance — with a clear migration path to global-scale infrastructure as the ecosystem grows.

Development stack

React.jsx and Express.js power the web platform. Kotlin and Jetpack Compose drive the Android app. iOS in Swift and SwiftUI is currently in development — following Android's launch on Google Play.

React.jsx Express.js Kotlin Jetpack Compose Swift / SwiftUI

Hosting & deployment

WhoGoHost powers regional deployment ensuring low-latency performance for West African users. AWS or GCP migration is planned as the ecosystem scales to a wider user base.

WhoGoHost AWS · GCP

CREDA AI engine

Powered by Google Gemini API, CREDA delivers predictive stocking, churn risk detection, and upsell intelligence. Conversations supported in Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, Fon, Gun, and other major West African languages.

Google Gemini API Predictive analytics Multilingual

Data architecture

Supabase serves as the central cloud data layer. Room DB enables offline-first mobile storage. WorkManager handles background synchronisation — ensuring resilience across variable connectivity.

Supabase Room DB WorkManager

Identity & trust

Termii handles OTP authentication across the platform. Smile Identity will power KYC and KYB verification — bringing verified trust to every business and seller profile in the ecosystem.

Termii OTP Smile Identity

Payments infrastructure

Flutterwave serves as the default payment gateway — supporting NGN, XOF, and cross-border transactions across West Africa, with compatibility for MTN MoMo, Moov Money, and other regional payment methods.

Flutterwave NGN · XOF Cross-border
